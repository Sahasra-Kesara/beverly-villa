About Beverly villa

Beverly Villa is a luxurious colonial-style villa located just 200 meters from the stunning Mount Lavinia Beach. With its prime location, guests can enjoy the breathtaking ocean views and soak up the sun on the white sand beaches.

The villa boasts spacious and elegantly decorated rooms, each with its own unique character and charm. Guests will have access to all the modern amenities they need for a comfortable stay, including air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs.
